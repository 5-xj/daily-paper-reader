## 摘要
真核生物基因组的三维结构由分区（compartments）、拓扑相关域（TADs）和环（loops）组成，这些结构由构架蛋白介导，不同物种中的组织原则有所差异。在果蝇中，绝缘子蛋白（如 Su(Hw)）以及组蛋白变体 {gamma}H2Av 能形成液-液相分离（LLPS）凝聚体，但其相分离能力与基因组分区的关系尚不明确。本文采用高渗透压胁迫同时使果蝇和人类细胞中的构架蛋白从染色质上脱离，从而进行跨物种的基因组组织原则比较分析。我们发现，尽管人类 CTCF 具有与果蝇绝缘子蛋白类似的预测 LLPS 特性，但它在渗透压胁迫下并不形成凝聚体，而果蝇的绝缘子蛋白则会形成凝聚体。Hi-C 分析显示，渗透压胁迫在两种生物中均导致分区、TAD 边界强度和环结构的丧失；然而，人类细胞在胁迫后基因组恢复迅速并几乎完全，而果蝇在一个小时后仍然恢复不完全。对这种恢复不对称性的分析揭示了分区组织的根本性差异：人类的 A 与 B 分区之间存在强的同型长程相互作用，而果蝇的 B 分区很少参与长程的 B-B 接触，表明果蝇基因组并不复制典型的 A/B 分区架构。相反，果蝇的基因组结构主要由 A-A 相互作用主导，且 A 分区中特异性富集 {gamma}H2Av 和 Su(Hw)，并有适度的 cohesin 亚基富集。此外，果蝇中的环结构在机制上独立于分区和 TAD，它们在分区结构恢复之前即已恢复，并由 Su(Hw) 和 cohesin 锚定，而非 dCTCF。综合来看，这些发现表明，{gamma}H2Av 和 Su(Hw) 的液-液相分离特性通过一种不同于脊椎动物中由异染色质驱动、以 B 分区为主的机制，介导果蝇中 A 分区的形成，揭示了果蝇与人类基因组之间根本不同的组织原则。

## Abstract
The three-dimensional organization of eukaryotic genomes into compartments, topologically associating domains, and loops is mediated by architectural proteins whose organizational principles vary across species. In Drosophila, insulator proteins including Su(Hw) and the histone variant {gamma}H2Av form liquid-liquid phase separation (LLPS) condensates, yet how this phase separation capacity relates to genome compartmentalization has remained unclear. Here we use hyperosmotic stress to simultaneously displace architectural proteins from chromatin in both Drosophila and human cells, enabling a comparative dissection of genome organization principles across species. We find that although human CTCF shares predicted LLPS properties with Drosophila insulator proteins, it does not form condensates upon osmotic stress, while Drosophila insulator proteins do. Hi-C analysis reveals that osmotic stress causes loss of compartments, TAD boundary strength, and loops in both organisms, but genome recovery after stress is rapid and near-complete in human cells while remaining substantially incomplete in Drosophila after one hour. Analysis of this recovery asymmetry reveals a fundamental difference in compartment organization: whereas human A and B compartments engage in robust homotypic long-range interactions, Drosophila B compartments rarely participate in long-range B-to-B contacts, indicating that the Drosophila genome does not replicate canonical A/B compartment organization. Instead, Drosophila genome architecture is dominated by A-to-A interactions, and A compartments are specifically enriched in {gamma}H2Av and Su(Hw), with moderate enrichment of cohesin subunits. Furthermore, loops in Drosophila are mechanistically independent from compartments and TADs, recovering before compartment structure is restored, and are anchored by Su(Hw) and cohesin rather than by dCTCF. Together, these findings suggest that the LLPS properties of {gamma}H2Av and Su(Hw) underlie A compartment formation in Drosophila through a mechanism distinct from the heterochromatin-driven B compartment interactions that predominate in vertebrates, revealing fundamentally different organizational principles between fly and human genomes.

## 六、主要结论与发现

1. **跨物种应激响应**  
   - 高渗压均导致染色质压缩、短程接触减少、长程接触增加；
   - 果蝇绝缘子形成 LLPS 凝聚体，而人类 CTCF 不形成。  

2. **基因组三维结构重组特征**
   - 两者均失去清晰的 A/B compartment pattern、TAD 边界和loops；
   - 但人类细胞在恢复 1 小时后大部分结构恢复，果蝇恢复明显滞后。  

3. **分区组织原则有根本差异**  
   - **人类基因组**：兼具强 A-A 与 B-B 长程同型相互作用（典型 A/B 分区）；
   - **果蝇基因组**：主要由 A-A 相互作用维持，B-B 相互作用极弱，不呈典型交替结构。  

4. **驱动机制差异**  
   - 果蝇中 A 区域富集 γH2Av 与 Su(Hw)，二者具 LLPS 能力，与 compartment 形成密切相关；
   - 人类分区重建依赖 heterochromatin (B compartments) 及 loop extrusion 机制。  

5. **结构层次独立性**  
   - Drosophila 中 loops 与 TAD/Compartments 去耦合：loops 在恢复期比 TAD 更快恢复；
   - 果蝇 loops 由 Su(Hw) 与 cohesin（非 dCTCF）锚定。

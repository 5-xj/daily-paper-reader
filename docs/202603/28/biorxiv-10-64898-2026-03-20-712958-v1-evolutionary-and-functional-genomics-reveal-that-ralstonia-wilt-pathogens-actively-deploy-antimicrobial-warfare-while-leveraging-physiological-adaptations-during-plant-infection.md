## 摘要
青枯雷尔氏菌种群（RSSC）中的木质部病原体引起萎蔫病，威胁全球粮食安全。这些多样化的病原体高度适应植物体内环境，并在其中增殖并导致快速、侵略性的疾病。为了了解这些病原体在植物体内适应性的遗传基础，我们利用高通量随机条形码转座子测序（RB-TnSeq）对三种 RSSC 物种进行了正向遗传筛选。我们量化了数十万个突变体在感病番茄植株茎部定殖过程中的竞争适应性。我们将这种植物体内正向遗传筛选与之前在还原论、类植物环境中进行的筛选（即从健康番茄植株中收获的离体木质部汁液）进行了比较。比较遗传筛选揭示了使 RSSC 致病成功的保守和谱系特异性同源基因。一致地，这些多样化的病原体通过维持具有弹性的细胞包被屏障、补充木质部汁液中的营养可用性、微调其对宿主操纵型 III 型分泌系统的利用，以及通过一套环境感知和转录调节蛋白动态调节其基因表达，来应对植物体内的机遇和压力。令人惊讶的是，菌株特异性的适应性因子揭示了病原体主导感病宿主之外的生态相互作用，即使在单菌株感染期间，病原体也动用了抗微生物武器库。特别是在植物体内生长期间，每个 RSSC 菌株都需要一套独特的 VI 型分泌系统免疫蛋白，为其自身的毒素提供自我保护。我们通过将 RSSC 青枯病原体与其非致病近缘种进行演化基因组比较，对植物体内适应性因子进行了背景化分析。总之，这些分析揭示了保守和谱系特异性的适应性决定因素是如何演化以支持植物维管束生态位中的致病成功的。

## Abstract
The xylem pathogens in the Ralstonia solanacearum species complex (RSSC) cause wilt diseases that threaten global food security. These diverse pathogens are highly adapted to the in planta environment where they proliferate and cause rapid, aggressive diseases. To understand the genetic underpinnings of these pathogens in planta fitness, we performed forward genetic screens in three RSSC species, using high-throughput random barcode transposon sequencing (RB-TnSeq). We quantified the competitive fitness of hundreds of thousands of mutants during stem colonization of susceptible tomato plants. We compared this in planta forward genetic screen with a prior forward genetic screen performed in a reductionist, plant-like environment: ex vivo xylem sap harvest from healthy tomato plants. The comparative genetic screens revealed both conserved and lineage-specific orthologs that enable RSSCs pathogenic success. Consistently, these diverse pathogens navigated the opportunities and stressors in planta by maintaining a resilient cell envelope barrier, complementing the nutritional availability in xylem sap, fine-tuning their utilization of the host-manipulating type III secretion system, and dynamically regulating their gene expression through a suite of environment sensing and transcriptional regulatory proteins. Surprisingly, the strain-specific fitness factors shed light on ecological interactions beyond pathogen dominance of their susceptible host, even during the single-strain infections, the pathogens wielded arsenals of anti-microbial weapons. Specifically during growth in planta, each RSSC strain required a unique repertoire of type VI secretion system immunity proteins that provide self-protection to their own toxins. We contextualized the in planta fitness factors through evolutionary genomic comparisons of the RSSC wilt pathogens to their non-pathogenic neighbors. Together, these analyses reveal how conserved and lineage-specific fitness determinants have evolved to support pathogenic success in the plant vascular niche.

#### 7. 优点
*   **多菌株视角**：克服了以往研究仅关注单一模型菌株（如 GMI1000）的局限，揭示了种群内的遗传异质性。
*   **环境对比**：通过植物体内 vs 离体汁液的对比，精准定位了受宿主诱导防御影响的基因。
*   **演化深度**：将功能基因组数据与大规模系统发育分析结合，提供了关于致病性起源的深刻见解。

#### 8. 不足与局限
*   **宿主单一性**：实验主要在番茄上完成，对于 RSSC 广泛的宿主范围（如土豆、香蕉等）是否存在不同的适应性逻辑尚待验证。
